About us

This web application was created as a project for Eindhoven University of Technology. The team consists of Feng Gao, Sjoerd Offringa, Lisa Rabbers, Rares Rădulescu and Daniël van der Valk. The members come from different specializations providing different points of view to the subject of data visualization. The database that is used here belongs to Enron, an energy providing company that filed for bankruptcy due to accounting fraud. The database consists of the email communication between the employees.
